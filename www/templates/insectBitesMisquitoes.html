Warnings & Information

Use Ledum palustre prior to exposure to decrease the swelling and itching and Calendula Gel after exposure to relieve itching. Do not scratch the wound to avoid superinfection. These symptoms may indicate West Nile fever or malaria--if you live or travel in an area in which malaria is endemic--or another infection.
